## SeaSurfaceTemperatures
+plot_ersst5.py``` : example of the interannual seasonal cycle of sea surface temperatures (SST) using the new ERSSTv5 data set. Selected years are arbitrary, but 1992-2016 (monthly) for the example. Output includes (1) png file per loop.

## Temperature
+ plot_ArcticTemperatures_Reanalysis.py : script plots a time series of the mean (2-m) air temperature (67N+) from 1958 to 2016 using various reanalysis data sets. Data sets in this example include: NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis 1 (R1), NCEP-DOE AMIP-II Reanalysis (R2), Climate Forecast System Reanalysis (CFSR), Modern-Era Retrospective analysis for Research and Applications, Version 2  (MERRA-2), Japanese 55-year Reanalysis (JRA-55), and ERA-Interim (ERAi). Data is provided in the ```Data``` directory.
+ plot_rankmesh_NCEP.py : script ranks the mean Arctic (70N+) air temperature at 925 hPa over the satellite era (monthly from 1979). For example, a rank of 39 is the 'coldest' month in the time series and a rank of 1 is the 'warmest' month. Data is from NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis 1 (R1) and provided in the ```Data``` directory. 

## Utilities
+ plot_80N_circle.py : script plots the region of averaging for the ECMWF 80N+ (2-m) temperature figures (e.g., FDD, TDD). Area includes both land and ocean.
